Ruier Seal — The Best Security Seals

Company Introduction

Shandong Ruier Seal Co., Ltd. is committed to the development of the seal industry for one time used security seals. It specializes in the research, development, manufacture and sale of seals and anti-theft products. MAIN PRODUCTS: CONTAINER BOLT SEALS, PLASTIC SEALS, CABLE SEALS, METER SEALS, METAL STRAP SEALS AND OTHERS. The company's products are widely used in electricity meter, water table, postal bag, power supply, gas supply, oil transportation, chemical industry, mine, postal service, customs, railway, finance, container, transportation products processing, etc.

Our factory is located in Qingyun County, Dezhou City, Shandong Province. We have more than 230 employees, 16 senior technical staff, research and design staff 10, 28 mid-level technical staff, with more than 2600 million yuan in fixed assets, working capital of more than 4200 million.
